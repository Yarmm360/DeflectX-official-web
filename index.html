    About

    Most Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tools are built for enterprise budgets and massive infrastructure. They require complex cloud deployments, heavy storage configurations, and endless sign-up forms just to get started. For a student, a hobbyist, or a junior SOC analyst, the barrier to entry is simply too high.

    This project changes that.

    We believe that learning cybersecurity should be hands-on, immediate, and accessible to everyone. This is a fully open-source, beginner-friendly SIEM designed to deploy just like any standard desktop software.

    • No Sign-ups or Paywalls: Just download, install, and start analyzing.
    • Zero Cloud Dependencies: It runs entirely locally on your machine—no expensive cloud storage or complex architecture required.
    • Built for Learners: A clean, intuitive interface that helps you understand event correlation and log analysis without getting buried in enterprise bloat.

    Whether you are spinning up your first home lab or trying to map out your first detection rules, this SIEM gives you the power of log management without the setup headache.
